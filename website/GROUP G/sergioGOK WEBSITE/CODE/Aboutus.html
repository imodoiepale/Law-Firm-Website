LEGAL PRACTITIONERS

With over 30 years of combined legal practice, the Law Office of Clinton Consultancy provides legal, transaction advisory and project facilitation services. We also undertake bespoke and high profile reputation management projects throughout Africa. In terms of legal work, we provide litigation and fraud recovery services whilst offering clients in-depth advice on corporate and commercial transactions, public-private partnerships, commercial negotiations and arbitrations, incorporation and registration of business entities, investments and many other corporate processes. In providing legal services to both local and international clients, we have presence in Ghana, and a network in Sierra-Leone, South-Africa, Egypt, Angola and Nigeria in particular.

All partners were trained in the U.K and their African country of base with each partner having been involved in major transactions throughout Africa particularly in the fields of project finance, oil and gas, mergers and acquisitions and mining. Over the last 10 years, we have worked with some of the world’s leading businesses on transactions executed in Africa and assist in a number of fraud related matters throughout Africa including online romance scams and gold related scams.

At Clinton Consultancy, we pride ourselves in bringing new and original ways of thinking to the complex challenges our clients face. Recognized for our industry focus, we are strong across all the key industry sectors including financial institutions, energy (upstream and downstream), banking and finance, corporate and commercial, mergers and acquisitions, natural resources, tax, corporate governance and compliance, estate and land transactions and wealth management, infrastructure, mining and commodities, transport, technology and innovation.
